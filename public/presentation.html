The Problem

Assessments tell us the score,
but not the reason behind mistakes.

❌ Current System

Score: 6/10

The student got 4 wrong. But which concept is the student struggling with? Nobody knows.

❌ The Hidden Mistake

Student adds fractions wrong

The system marks it wrong but can't identify the root cause misconception.

½ + ⅓ = 2/5 ← Added denominators!
✅ Our Solution

Identify the exact "Learning Gap" & act on it immediately

Our AI doesn't just mark wrong — it diagnoses the misconception, then intervenes with an AI tutor in real-time and generates targeted practice worksheets for the teacher.
